---
title: How do you handle stress?
---

Software development, especially in the frontend, is a rapidly changing field. I believe that thoughtfully analyzing and handling challenges without haste is the best way to ease stress.

<br />

When faced with stress, I always start by confirming the boundaries of the problem and then eliminate issues one by one. The process is similar to how I manage my <a class="underline" href="https://www.webdong.dev/post/">blog</a>. In fact, many of the challenges I encounter at work eventually become articles on my blog. This not only helps me organize my thoughts but also assists others.
